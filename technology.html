Technology

The Waveline Magnet represents a revolutionary approach to harnessing the power of ocean waves. Comprising an array of flexible assemblies interconnected by a spine power system, this patented technology is designed to seamlessly integrate with the natural motion of the waves.

Unlike traditional wave energy converters, the Waveline Magnet allows the wave to pass through its system, effectively capturing the kinetic energy as the wave rises and falls. This innovative design ensures maximum efficiency in energy extraction while minimizing disruption to the surrounding marine environment.

Central to the technology is its ability to synchronize with the natural rhythm of the waves. By embracing the surface of the sea, or the 'wave line', as it is commonly referred to by NWEL, the Waveline Magnet becomes one moving mass with the wave itself. This unique interaction enables NWEL to precisely regulate the amount of energy extracted from the wave in a controlled and non-disruptive manner.

The seamless and frictionless nature of this interaction is a defining characteristic of the technology. It allows the Waveline Magnet to operate in perfect harmony with the deployed sea area, adapting effortlessly to varying wave profiles and weather conditions.
