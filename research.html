Research

Nebula Wave Energy Ltd (NWEL) has been diligently conducting groundbreaking research and development behind the scenes, and now it's time to unveil their remarkable innovations to the world. The journey began in 2006 with the inception of the first Waveline Magnet device. Since then, NWEL's dedicated team has iterated upon its designs, pushing the boundaries of wave energy technology with each new model.

Located in Rio de Janeiro, NWEL enjoys convenient access to offshore facilities, particularly in BÃºzios, where they have conducted extensive sea trials for each iteration of the Waveline Magnet. These trials have been instrumental in accelerating the research and development process, allowing NWEL to refine their designs and validate their performance in real-world conditions.

Prototype being tested in the open sea in 2012

Over the course of more than a decade of comprehensive R&D efforts, NWEL has achieved a significant milestone with the development of the groundbreaking Wave Line Magnet (WLM). This revolutionary technology represents the culmination of years of innovation and dedication, addressing the challenges faced by traditional wave energy converters.

The Wave Line Magnet offers a comprehensive solution to the current limitations of wave energy conversion, ensuring efficient, economical, and sustainable power production for a wide range of applications. With NWEL's commitment to excellence and continuous improvement, the WLM stands poised to revolutionize the renewable energy landscape.
