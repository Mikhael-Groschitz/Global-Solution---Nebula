About Us

Welcome to Nebula Wave Energy Ltd (NWEL), an innovative Research and Development (R&D) company headquartered in Brazil. Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize renewable energy through the creation of cutting-edge wave energy converters (WECs), particularly our flagship product, the “Wave Line Magnet”.

The Wave Line Magnet represents more than a decade of relentless dedication to research and development. Since our inception, we have been singularly focused on refining and perfecting this technology, resulting in numerous patents and accolades. Our WEC is not just a device; it's a testament to our commitment to sustainability and innovation.

At NWEL, we pride ourselves on producing WECs that are robust, durable, and highly efficient. Our technology is designed to harness the immense power of ocean waves, providing a reliable and cost-effective source of clean energy. With minimal maintenance requirements and the ability to operate in diverse wave environments, our WECs offer a sustainable solution to the world's energy needs.

Come and Join Us in Shaping a Sustainable Future

NWEL has been quietly working behind the scenes, but we are now ready to share our vision with the world. Our journey began in 2006 with the conception of the first Wave Line Magnet device. Since then, we have continuously evolved and iterated, incorporating lessons learned and pushing the boundaries of wave energy technology.

We invite you to join us on this exciting journey towards a brighter, more sustainable future. Together, we can harness the power of the ocean to create a world powered by clean, renewable energy—one wave at a time.
