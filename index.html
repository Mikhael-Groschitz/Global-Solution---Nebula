Nebula Wave Energy Ltd

Nebula Wave Energy Ltd (NWEL) is an R&D company based in Brazil that has been focused on the design and development of its wave energy converter (WEC) – The “Wave Line Magnet”, a wave energy converter that has been evolving for more than 10 years, achieving numerous patents. The company's technology is a robust and durable WEC that can supply substantial power on demand at a low cost, with minimal maintenance, and can be deployed in any wave environment.
